An educational game designed for Niceville High School students to practice polynomial equations in a fun, competitive format.

## About

Students compete in teams answering polynomial questions. Correct answers move teams forward, while incorrect answers move them back. First team to reach position 20 wins!

## Features

- 26 polynomial questions (multiple choice and short answer)
- 2-4 team support
- No question repeats within a game
- Special themed spaces (Eglin AFB, Festival, etc.)
- Shows correct answers when students get questions wrong
- Mobile-friendly design

## How to Play

1. Enter 2-4 team names
2. Teams take turns drawing questions
3. Answer correctly to move forward 2 spaces
4. Wrong answers move you back 2 spaces
5. First to position 20 (or beyond) wins!
